Problem's name:

Plant's problems

Root and stem rots

Symptoms

Brown to black soft or punky roots; Gridled soft stems with a brown or black ring near the soil line; Plants wilt and eventually die

Management

punky roots; Gridled soft stems with a brown or black ring near the soil line; Plants wilt and eventually die Avoid overwatering; Remove infected plants;
Where symptoms are infecting some but not all the roots, cut out infected roots, then repot plant using sterile potting mix and a clean pot
